Frequently Asked Questions

How do we start a project collaboration?

Simply contact us via the form below or give us a call. We'll assign a senior project manager to discuss your requirements, assess the scope, and provide a complimentary preliminary quotation.

Do you accept test projects?

For long-term, large-scale collaborations, we do accept paid test projects. If we proceed with full collaboration, the test fee can be deducted from the final project cost.

How is intellectual property handled?

This is paramount to us. Upon full payment, all source files and intellectual property rights transfer completely to you. We operate under strict Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) to protect your assets.

What are your typical project turnaround times?

Timelines vary based on project complexity. A single high-quality 3D asset may take 1-2 weeks, whilst a full game art package or animation could span several months. We'll provide a detailed schedule during the briefing phase.

Can you work with our existing creative team?

Absolutely! We frequently collaborate with in-house teams, providing supplementary capacity, specialised expertise, or white-label production services. We seamlessly integrate into your existing workflows.
